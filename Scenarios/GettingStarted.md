# Getting Started

## Prerequisites

- Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central (v28.0 or later).
- An Azure Application Insights resource connected to your Business Central
  environment.
- The **AI Telemetry** app installed from AppSource.
- Appropriate permissions (see [Permissions](Permissions.md)).

## First-time setup

1. Open the **AI Telemetry Setup** page via the search bar.
2. Enter your Application Insights **App ID** and choose an authentication
   method (API Key or Client Credentials).
3. Optionally enable Copilot integration (requires Copilot capabilities to be
   active in your environment).
4. Choose **Test App Insights Connection** to verify connectivity.

![AI Telemetry Setup](../Assets/screenshot_07_setup.png)

Once the setup is complete you can start querying telemetry immediately.
