# What does AI Telemetry do?

AI Telemetry brings Azure Application Insights telemetry data directly into
Business Central. Instead of switching to the Azure portal or writing complex KQL
queries manually, you can:

- **Query telemetry with natural language** — describe what you want to find and
  Copilot generates the KQL for you.
- **Execute KQL queries** — run queries against your Application Insights
  workspace without leaving Business Central.
- **Manage a library of saved queries** — store, organise, and reuse queries that
  matter to your organisation.
- **Use parameterised queries** — define parameters with lookup values for
  flexible, reusable query templates.
- **Review query results** — view results in a structured grid with detail
  drill-down.

## Who is it for?

- **Administrators** who monitor environment health and performance.
- **Consultants** who troubleshoot issues using telemetry signals.
- **Developers** who validate custom telemetry during testing.
- **Business users** who need visibility into business process failures without using Azure tools.
